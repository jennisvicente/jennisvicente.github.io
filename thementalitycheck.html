Thoughts of An Empowered Woman

My name is Jennis and I am the creator of Undateable: Is this really dating?

I started the journey of documenting my dating life after divorce. The purpose was to portray my views in dating methods and share an analysis of my experiences based on what I believed in and society expectations. By doing this, I was also getting in touch with my true self. It made me grow as a person and change the way I see different situations.

My ultimate desire was to find love and I wanted to piece together my journey to it. In that page I poured out different emotions. My style is a bit sarcastic and it is meant to help other ladies out there that, like me, were having a hard time being understood.

I recently found the love of my life. We fell in love very fast… So much that I can say I couldn’t bear a day without his presence. Until today. Today I was going to dump the man of my life because I am a woman. Not just any woman, an empowered woman. Someone who would put loving myself before anything else. A woman that believes I deserve to be respected as a human. A woman that can not tolerate any degree of misogyny and will not blame the environment or justify that mentality. Who was smart enough to understand there has to be willingness to change a point of view. And there’s also willingness to stand your ground and get your point across and actually stimulate a change.

I’ve realized that I have the power to revert an ancient mentality by sharing the importance of what I believe in. So I decided to write to inspire that change.

“We but mirror the world. All the tendencies present in the outer world are to be found in the world of our body. If we could change ourselves, the tendencies in the world would also change. As a man changes his own nature, so does the attitude of the world change towards him. This is the divine mystery supreme. A wonderful thing it is and the source of our happiness. We need not wait to see what others do.” – Mahatma Gandhi

In order for the world to change. I have to be the change. So this is the beginning.
